How I build

Speed matters. Trust compounds.

Good products are not only impressive demos. They are understandable, testable, and honest about their limits.

  1. 01

    Start with evidence

    Define the user, the painful workflow, and the smallest outcome worth testing before choosing a stack.

  2. 02

    Automate the repeatable

    Let software handle discovery, organization, and analysis while people retain judgment over meaningful actions.

  3. 03

    Ship with guardrails

    Prefer local processing, least privilege, deterministic tests, and explicit failure modes over hidden magic.

  4. 04

    Learn from reality

    Measure whether the workflow saves time, improves decisions, or creates value—then keep, change, or stop.
